All non-hazardous wastes require collection and transport to a processing or disposal site. 
Such wastes are also known as REFUSE or municipal solid waste.

Refuse includes Garbage, rubbish, trash and e-waste.

GARBAGE are mostly decompoable food waste.
RUBBISH are mostly dry materials such as glass, paper, cloth or wood.
TRASH are bulky rubbish items such as refrigerators, couches or large tree stumps.
Electronic WASTE includes discarded computer equipments, televisions, telephones,etc.
